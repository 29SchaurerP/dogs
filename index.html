Popular Dog Breeds

Here are some well-known dog breeds and what they are known for:

Working and Sporting Breeds

  • Labrador Retriever – Friendly, energetic, and great family dogs.
  • German Shepherd – Intelligent and often used as service or police dogs.
  • Golden Retriever – Gentle, loyal, and very trainable.

Small Companion Breeds

  1. Pug – Playful and affectionate with a funny personality.
  2. Beagle – Curious, active, and great with kids.
  3. Yorkshire Terrier – Small, bold, and full of energy.

Basic Dog Care Tips

To keep a dog healthy and happy, owners should focus on these key areas:

  • Nutrition: Provide high-quality dog food and fresh water.
  • Exercise: Daily walks and playtime to burn energy.
  • Grooming: Regular brushing, nail trimming, and baths.
  • Veterinary Care: Yearly checkups and vaccinations.
  • Training: Positive reinforcement and clear rules.
